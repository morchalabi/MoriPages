About Me

I am a computational biologist with a strong background in AI and machine learning for biomedical applications. I specialize in integrating multimodal data, including single-cell and spatial omics, to advance precision oncology, particularly in gastrointestinal cancers. With a Ph.D. in Biomathematics and a decade of experience, my work has been published in journals like Nature Genetics and eLife. I am also an experienced educator committed to training the next generation of biomedical data scientists.
